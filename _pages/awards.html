2025 National Merit Finalist, February 2025

  • The National Merit Scholarship Corporation (NMSC) conducts the annual National Merit Scholarship Program.

  • The national-wide pool of finalists, representing less than 1% of US High School Seniors, includes the highest scoring entrants in each state.
